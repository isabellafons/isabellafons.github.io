TEDxYouth @OTHS


Talk | April 24th, 2017

Overview


On April 24th, 2017, I was newly 17 and was given the opportunity to fulfill one of my biggest goals: giving a TEDx talk. It was something I never thought could happen, but my high school NHS president decided to host a TEDxYouth event at our school and I was fortunate to recieve the opportunity to speak. Inspired by the quote from John Steinbeck's East of Eden , "Now that you don't have to be perfect, you can be good," my talked focused on how steering away from perfectionism can lead to more joy, happiness, and fulfillment, especially in the lucrative sport that is women's running. To my surprise, the talk has gathered over 360,000 views. Although the talk is now nearing 4 year olds, it will always holds a special place in my heart.
