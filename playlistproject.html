Overview


I am absoultely in love with making Spotify playlists. It's how I relax, work through my emotions, and even express creativity. Sounds dramatic, but I am dramatic and I also just really love music. Inspired by Spotify Wrapped—which provides personalized, annual insights on listening hisory every December—I decided to do a deep analysis of the playlists I created in 2020. I completed this project using the spotifyR package created by Charlie Thompson.
